Palawan



About the province: Palawan is an island in Luzon. It is popular for its beaches and caves. There are many municipalities and villages. Howeve, there are three known for its waters: Coron, El Nido, and Puerto Princesa.



Activities: Boat Sailing, Diving, Island Hopping, Kayak, Swimming, etc.

Warning: Bring your own water because the local water may be contaminated.
